Why send a parcel with UltraShip Express?

With UltraShip Express, you can quickly post a parcel across the whole of the UK and beyond. UK prices start at £2.62! Even when choosing our standard delivery service, you’ll get immediate access to the following parcel service benefits:

Complete delivery flexibility

Post a package using any one of our 14,000+ ParcelShops and Lockers across the UK or book a delivery straight from your door with our Courier Collection service, no matter where you are in the country.


A quality delivery service

With our standard parcel service, you get full tracking on every delivery, photo confirmation of your parcel’s arrival, and free cover up to a value of £20 as standard. We’ll even provide you with a 1-hour window for both collection and delivery, with our Couriers making up to 3 delivery attempts before diverting your parcel to your nearest ParcelShop.


A cheaper parcel service

We’ll always aim to give you the best prices when you post a parcel with UltraShip Express, regardless of whether you choose to use our ParcelShops, Lockers, or Courier Collection Service.

Our Courier service

When using UltraShip Express Courier service to send or return a parcel, you’ll get full tracking included in the cost of postage.

On the day of your parcel delivery, you’ll get a 1-hour delivery slot. This means you can plan your day around us.

If you’re not going to be in, don’t worry! Our tracking tool lets you divert your parcel or select a ‘Safe Place’ for our parcel Couriers to leave your items.

Cheap parcel returns

Bought something you need to send back? With UltraShip Express, you can get cheap parcel returns for many of the biggest UK retailers. Some even offer free returns.

Just return your parcels using our ParcelShops or Lockers. Alternatively, book a local Courier to collect the package from your home or office.

Need to print a postage label for your UltraShip Express return? Head to your nearest ParcelShop with a Print in ParcelShop Device. Then, print and return your package in one place!

Choose your parcel delivery option

When sending a parcel with UltraShip Express, you can choose from either of our two cheap parcel services – standard delivery or Next Day delivery:

Standard delivery from £30.62

Our equivalent of sending a parcel second class with Royal Mail, you can use any of our ParcelShops, Lockers, or our Courier Collection Service to get your parcel delivered in 2 to 3 days.

Next Day delivery from £58.20

Drop your parcel off at your nearest ParcelShop or Locker by 12pm on a weekday and we’ll do our best to deliver it for the following working day when you choose our Next Day delivery service.
